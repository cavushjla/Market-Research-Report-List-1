<p><p>The linear heat detector market encompasses two primary types: fiber optic and cable-based systems. Fiber optic detectors utilize optical fibers to detect temperature changes along their length, offering high sensitivity and immunity to electromagnetic interference. In contrast, cable-based linear heat detectors consist of specialized cables that change resistance in response to temperature variations, making them suited for various environments. Both systems provide continuous monitoring and early fire detection, ensuring safety in commercial, industrial, and residential applications.</p></p>
