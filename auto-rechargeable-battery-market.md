<p><p>The auto rechargeable battery market consists of various types, each with unique characteristics. Nickel-Cadmium (NiCd) batteries offer durability but suffer from memory effect. Nickel-Metal Hydride (NiMH) batteries provide higher capacity and are more environmentally friendly. Lithium-Ion batteries are popular for their lightweight and high energy density. Lithium-Polymer batteries, a variation of Li-ion, are flexible in shape and offer enhanced safety. Lead-Acid batteries, while heavier, are cost-effective and widely used in automotive applications due to their reliability.</p></p>
