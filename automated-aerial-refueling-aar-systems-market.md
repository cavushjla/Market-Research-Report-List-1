<p><ul><li>Probe and Drogue Type</li><li>Boom and Receptacle Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automated Aerial Refueling (AAR) systems are crucial for extending the operational range of military aircraft. The market is primarily divided into two types: Probe and Drogue, and Boom and Receptacle. The Probe and Drogue type uses a flexible hose and drogue basket, allowing versatile refueling angles, beneficial for smaller aircraft. Conversely, the Boom and Receptacle system features a rigid boom that connects directly to the receiving aircraft, providing high transfer rates and stability, preferred for larger military planes during refueling operations.</p></p>
