<p><p>The Biological Sample Drug Detector market is primarily categorized into two types: saliva detection and hair detection. Saliva detection involves analyzing oral fluid to identify recent drug use, offering rapid results and non-invasive collection. This method is often used for roadside testing and workplace screenings. Hair detection, on the other hand, analyzes hair samples to ascertain drug use over an extended period, typically providing a history of substance exposure. Both methods serve vital roles in drug testing, public safety, and compliance monitoring.</p></p>
