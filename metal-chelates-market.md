<p><p>The metal chelates market can be categorized into three main types: essential nutrients, auxiliary nutrients, and micronutrients. Essential nutrients are crucial for plant growth and development, encompassing elements like iron, copper, and zinc that are typically found in chelated forms for improved bioavailability. Auxiliary nutrients support overall plant health but are not directly involved in primary growth processes. Micronutrients, although needed in smaller quantities, play a critical role in enzymatic functions and metabolic processes, enhancing crop yield and quality.</p></p>
