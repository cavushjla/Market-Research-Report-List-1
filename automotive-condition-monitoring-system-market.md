<p><p>The Automotive Condition Monitoring System market can be divided into two main types: Cloud Systems and Local Systems. Cloud Systems utilize remote servers to collect, store, and analyze data from vehicles, enabling real-time monitoring and access from anywhere via the internet. This enhances scalability and data sharing. In contrast, Local Systems rely on on-site data processing and storage within the vehicle or nearby infrastructure, providing quicker access to information but with limited external connectivity and scalability. Both systems aim to optimize vehicle maintenance and performance.</p></p>
