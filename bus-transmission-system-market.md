<p><p>The bus transmission system market comprises two primary types: manual and automatic transmission systems. Manual transmission systems require the driver to manually shift gears, providing greater control and typically better fuel efficiency. In contrast, automatic transmission systems shift gears automatically, offering convenience and ease of use, especially in urban settings. The choice between these systems affects driving experience, maintenance costs, and overall vehicle performance, influencing market demand and development trends in the bus industry.</p></p>
