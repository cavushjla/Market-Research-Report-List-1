<p><p>The ESD foam packaging market is primarily categorized into conductive and dissipative types, along with variations using metal and additives. Conductive foam enables the safe handling of sensitive electronic components by providing a path for static charge dissipation. Dissipative foam, while not a direct conductor, allows static charges to dissipate gradually. Additionally, metal-infused and additive-enhanced foams further improve performance by offering enhanced shielding and durability, effectively protecting electronic goods from electrostatic discharge during storage and transportation.</p></p>
