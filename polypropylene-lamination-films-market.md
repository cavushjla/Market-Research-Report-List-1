<p><p>Polypropylene lamination films are categorized by thickness, affecting their applications and performance. Films up to 0.1 mm are thin and flexible, ideal for lightweight packaging. The 0.1-0.5 mm range offers moderate durability, suitable for food and retail packaging. Thicknesses from 0.5 to 1 mm provide robust protection for industrial uses. Films above 1 mm are highly durable, often used in construction or heavy-duty applications. Each thickness serves distinct needs based on strength, flexibility, and barrier properties.</p></p>
