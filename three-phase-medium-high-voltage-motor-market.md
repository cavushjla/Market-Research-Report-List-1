<p><ul><li>Synchronous Motor</li><li>Asynchronous Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Three-Phase Medium and High Voltage Motor market comprises primarily two types: synchronous and asynchronous motors. Synchronous motors operate at a constant speed, synchronizing with the frequency of the supply current, making them ideal for applications requiring precise speed control. In contrast, asynchronous motors, also known as induction motors, operate at varying speeds based on load and are commonly used due to their simple design and reliability. Both types serve diverse industrial applications, enhancing efficiency and performance.</p></p>
