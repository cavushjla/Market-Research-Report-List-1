<p><p>The rigid foam polyether polyol market comprises several types, including sucrose-based, amino, and sorbitol-based polyether polyols. Sucrose-based polyether polyols are derived from sucrose and are valued for their efficiency in producing high-performance foams. Amino polyether polyols utilize amine chemistry, offering superior durability and thermal stability. Sorbitol-based polyether polyols are derived from sorbitol and are appreciated for their eco-friendliness and low toxicity, making them suitable for applications requiring sustainability while maintaining good foam properties.</p></p>
