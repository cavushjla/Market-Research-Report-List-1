<p><p>The Laser Extensometer market is primarily categorized into two types: built-in laser extensometers and frame laser extensometers. Built-in laser extensometers are integrated into testing systems, offering compact designs and ease of usage for monitoring strains in materials during experiments. In contrast, frame laser extensometers are standalone devices that provide flexibility and precision for measuring deformations, often used in laboratory settings. Both types utilize laser technology to deliver accurate and non-contact measurements for various engineering and materials testing applications.</p></p>
