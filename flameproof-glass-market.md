<p><p>The flameproof glass market is primarily categorized into two types: single-plate flameproof glass and compound-type flameproof glass. Single-plate flameproof glass consists of a single piece of tempered glass designed to withstand high temperatures and prevent flames from penetrating. In contrast, compound-type flameproof glass is composed of multiple layers, often incorporating a combination of glass and insulating materials, enhancing its resistance to heat and fire. Both types are essential for applications requiring safety and protection in high-risk environments.</p></p>
