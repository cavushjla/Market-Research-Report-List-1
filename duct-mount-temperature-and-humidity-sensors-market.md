<p><p>Duct mount temperature and humidity sensors are essential for monitoring air quality in HVAC systems. The market comprises various types, including capacitive and resistive sensors. Capacitive type sensors utilize changes in capacitance to measure humidity, offering high accuracy and stability. Resistive type sensors rely on changes in electrical resistance due to humidity variations, providing reliable performance at lower costs. Other sensor types may include thermal or hygrometric sensors, each catering to specific applications within building management and environmental control systems.</p></p>
