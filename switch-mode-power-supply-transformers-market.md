<p><p>Switch Mode Power Supply (SMPS) transformers are categorized into single-excited and double-excited types based on their winding configurations and operational characteristics. Single-excited transformers utilize a single primary winding to generate the required output, making them simpler and cost-effective. In contrast, double-excited transformers have both primary and secondary windings energized, allowing for higher efficiency and reduced electromagnetic interference. These variations cater to different applications in the electronics industry, influencing their market dynamics based on performance needs and cost considerations.</p></p>
