<p><p>The Outdoor Smart Locker market comprises two main types: contactless and contact. Contactless lockers utilize technology such as mobile apps or QR codes, allowing users to access their packages without physical interaction, enhancing convenience and safety. In contrast, contact lockers require users to interact directly with the locker interface, which may involve manual input or secure codes to retrieve items. Both types aim to streamline package delivery and collection, catering to varying consumer preferences and needs in outdoor settings.</p></p>
