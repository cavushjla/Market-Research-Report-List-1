<p><strong>The HLA Typing Transplant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Molecular Assays</li><li>Molecular Assays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HLA typing in the transplant market is categorized into two main types: non-molecular assays and molecular assays. Non-molecular assays include techniques like serological methods, which use antigen-antibody reactions to identify specific HLA types. In contrast, molecular assays utilize advanced techniques such as PCR and sequencing to provide precise and detailed genetic information about HLA genes. Both methods are essential in ensuring compatibility between donors and recipients, ultimately improving transplant success rates and minimizing rejection risks.</p></p>
