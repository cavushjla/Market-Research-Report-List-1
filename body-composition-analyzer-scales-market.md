<p><p>Body composition analyzer scales utilize various technologies to assess body composition. Bio-impedance analyzers measure resistance to electrical currents to estimate fat and lean mass. Dual-energy X-ray absorptiometry (DEXA) uses X-rays to provide detailed insights into bone density and body fat distribution. Air displacement plethysmography calculates body volume by measuring air displacement in a sealed chamber, offering accurate body density insights. Other methods may include skinfold calipers and ultrasound techniques, each catering to different preferences and accuracy requirements.</p></p>
