<p><p>The marine and boat batteries market features several types, including lithium batteries, fuel cell batteries, and lead-acid batteries. Lithium batteries are lightweight, offer high energy density, and have longer lifespans, making them popular for modern vessels. Fuel cell batteries convert chemical energy to electricity, providing efficient power with low emissions, suitable for eco-friendly boats. Lead-acid batteries are traditional, affordable, and reliable but heavier and less efficient, often used in older boats. Each type serves distinct needs depending on vessel requirements and usage.</p></p>
