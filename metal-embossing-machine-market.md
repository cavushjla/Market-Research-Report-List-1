<p><p>The metal embossing machine market is divided into two main types: manual and automatic embossing machines. Manual embossing machines require operators to physically guide the metal through the pressing process, offering greater control and customization for small-scale projects. In contrast, automatic embossing machines utilize advanced technology to perform high-speed, consistent embossing with minimal operator input, making them ideal for large-scale production. Each type caters to different production needs, ranging from artisanal craftsmanship to mass manufacturing efficiency.</p></p>
