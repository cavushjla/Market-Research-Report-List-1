<p><p>Chat applications are typically categorized into two market types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based chat applications are hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access them via the internet, offering scalability and flexibility without extensive infrastructure requirements. Conversely, on-premise chat applications are installed directly on a company’s servers, providing greater control over data and security but demanding higher initial investment and maintenance efforts. Both options cater to different organizational needs regarding accessibility, security, and management.</p></p>
