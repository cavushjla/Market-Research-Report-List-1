<p><p>The Non-contact Medical Infrared Thermometer market includes two primary types: forehead and ear thermometers. Forehead-type thermometers measure surface temperature from a distance, using infrared technology to ensure quick and non-invasive readings, ideal for all ages. Conversely, ear-type thermometers insert a probe into the ear canal to measure core body temperature, offering precise and swift results but generally recommended for children and adults. Both types have gained popularity for their convenience and reliability in fever detection.</p></p>
