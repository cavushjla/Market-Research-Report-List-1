<p><ul><li>Multi-point Borehole Extensometer</li><li>Single-point Borehole Extensometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The borehole extensometer market consists of multi-point and single-point types, used for monitoring ground movement and stability. Multi-point borehole extensometers feature multiple measurement points along a borehole, allowing for comprehensive data collection over different depths, enhancing accuracy in assessing subsurface deformations. In contrast, single-point borehole extensometers offer measurements from a single depth, providing a simpler and more cost-effective solution for specific applications. Both types are crucial for geological, civil engineering, and mining projects to ensure structural integrity and safety.</p></p>
