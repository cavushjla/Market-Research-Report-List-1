<p><p>The astronaut space suit market is primarily categorized into soft suits and hard shell suits. Soft suits are designed for flexibility and mobility, using lightweight materials for ease of movement during tasks in microgravity. In contrast, hard shell suits prioritize protection, incorporating rigid outer layers to shield astronauts from extreme temperatures and micrometeoroids during spacewalks. Each type serves specific mission requirements, balancing comfort and safety for astronauts in varying environments, from spacecraft operations to extravehicular activities.</p></p>
