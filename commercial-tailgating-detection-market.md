<p><p>The Commercial Tailgating Detection Market comprises two primary types: Imaging Measurement Technology and Non-Imaging Technology. Imaging Measurement Technology uses advanced cameras and sensors to capture visual data, enabling accurate detection of unauthorized entry in secure areas, often enhanced with AI for real-time analysis. In contrast, Non-Imaging Technology relies on methods like infrared sensors or pressure mats to identify tailgating incidents without visual monitoring. Both technologies aim to improve security by preventing unauthorized access in commercial facilities.</p></p>
