<p><p>The Antenna Surge Protector market encompasses three main types: Switch Type, Straight-through Type, and Composite Type. The Switch Type is designed to disconnect the circuit during a surge, providing protection while maintaining operational integrity. Straight-through Type ensures minimal signal loss by allowing signals to pass through unaffected, ideal for direct connections. Composite Type combines features of both, offering versatile protection for various applications. These types cater to different needs in safeguarding antennas from surges and voltage spikes.</p></p>
