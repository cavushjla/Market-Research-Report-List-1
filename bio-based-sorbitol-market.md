<p><p>The bio-based sorbitol market primarily includes two types: sorbitol liquid and sorbitol powder. Sorbitol liquid is a viscous solution, widely used in food and pharmaceuticals for its sweetening and humectant properties. It provides moisture retention in products. Sorbitol powder, on the other hand, is a dry, crystalline form that serves as a bulk sweetener and is often utilized in confectionery and dietary products. Both forms are derived from renewable sources, aligning with sustainability trends in various industries.</p></p>
