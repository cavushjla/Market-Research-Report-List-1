<p><p>The paint pigments market is primarily divided into two types: liquid and powder pigments. Liquid pigments are typically used in formulations like paints, varnishes, and inks, providing excellent dispersion and color intensity. They are favored for their ease of application and versatility. In contrast, powder pigments are solid, dry materials that require mixing with binders and solvents before use. They are commonly utilized in industrial applications, offering durability and rich color options. Each type serves distinct applications based on specific requirements.</p></p>
