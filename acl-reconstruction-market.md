<p><strong>The ACL Reconstruction Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tissue Fixation</li><li>Bone Fixation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ACL reconstruction involves two primary market types: tissue fixation and bone fixation. Tissue fixation encompasses various devices and methods used to secure the graft to the ligament, such as screws and staples, ensuring stability and proper healing. Bone fixation, on the other hand, focuses on anchoring the graft to the bone structure, utilizing techniques like interference screw fixation or cortical buttons. Both segments are critical in enhancing the success of ACL surgeries, contributing to improved patient outcomes and rehabilitation.</p></p>
