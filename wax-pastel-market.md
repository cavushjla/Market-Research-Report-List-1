<p><p>The wax pastel market comprises three primary types: paraffin, beeswax, and vegetable waxes. Paraffin wax, derived from petroleum, is widely used for its affordability and versatility in various applications. Beeswax, a natural product from honeybees, is prized for its unique texture and non-toxic properties, appealing to environmentally conscious consumers. Vegetable waxes, sourced from plants like soy or carnauba, offer sustainable alternatives and appeal to eco-friendly markets. Each type caters to distinct consumer preferences and creative applications in art and craft industries.</p></p>
