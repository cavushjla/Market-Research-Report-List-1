<p><p>The smart coffee machines market is primarily segmented into WiFi-enabled and Bluetooth-enabled coffee machines. WiFi-enabled coffee machines connect to home networks, allowing users to control brewing remotely via mobile apps, schedule brewing times, and receive alerts. In contrast, Bluetooth-enabled coffee machines provide direct connection to smartphones for personalized settings and adjustments, though their range is limited to the proximity of the device. Both types enhance user convenience and offer innovative features for a tailored coffee experience.</p></p>
