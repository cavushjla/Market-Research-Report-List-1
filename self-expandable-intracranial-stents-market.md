<p><p>Self-expandable intracranial stents are medical devices designed to treat intracranial artery stenosis and support vascular structures. Open-loop stents feature a design that allows for greater flexibility and adaptability to the arterial curvature, facilitating easier placement. Closed-loop stents, on the other hand, provide enhanced structural stability and are often preferred for areas requiring more support. Both types are pivotal in improving blood flow and reducing the risk of stroke by maintaining vessel patency in the neurovascular system.</p></p>
