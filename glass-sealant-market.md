<p><p>The glass sealant market is categorized into two primary types: single component and multi-component sealants. Single component sealants are ready-to-use products that cure upon exposure to air or moisture, making them convenient for quick applications. In contrast, multi-component sealants require mixing before application, allowing for greater customization and enhanced performance characteristics, such as improved adhesion or flexibility. Both types cater to specific requirements in construction, automotive, and industrial sectors, driving their demand in the market.</p></p>
