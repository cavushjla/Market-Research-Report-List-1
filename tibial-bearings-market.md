<p><strong>The Tibial Bearings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mobile-bearing</li><li>Fixed-bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tibial bearings market comprises two main types: mobile-bearing and fixed-bearing designs. Mobile-bearing tibial components allow for rotational movement, which can enhance the range of motion and reduce wear on the implant, making them suitable for younger, more active patients. Conversely, fixed-bearing tibial components are designed for stability and simpler surgical procedures, often favored for their reliability in older demographics. The choice between these types depends on patient activity levels, surgical preferences, and long-term performance expectations.</p></p>
